About Us

ORIECO Consulting K.K. is a private consulting company established in Tokyo, Japan, in 2015. We specialize in the energy and financial markets. With a professional team and partners, ORIECO has completed numerous consulting, research, advisory, capacity-building, and training projects in collaboration with both private and governmental entities.

Our Services

  1. Management Consulting: Business plans, strategic planning, M&A, alliances
  2. Bridge Consulting: Business and investment matchmaking
  3. Business Process Consulting: Reform, reorganization, outsourcing, process technology, plant planning
  4. Financial Solutions: Domestic and international financial services

Coverage

ORIECO covers a wide spectrum of industries and supports government policy. We offer end-to-end solutions from strategy to implementation. Our strengths lie in manufacturing, energy infrastructure, and finance. Projects have been successfully delivered in Australia, China, India, Japan, Thailand, and Vietnam.
