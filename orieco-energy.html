CET Brand: CPV Solar Cells

“WEDJAT” is representing the eye of the sun.

ORIECO Energy Development Co. is particularly strong in developing renewable energy projects including CPV Solar and Wind power generators. For solar, the exclusive brand is WEDJAT (Korean brand for CET CPV cells), and it is also the exclusive supplier of AVANTIS (wind power generators) from Germany to the Middle East.

The company also works in non-renewables like gas-based generators and has great networks and experience.

Joint Venture & CET Background

ORIECO Energy Development Co. is a joint venture formed by ORIECO International Co. Ltd. and CET Holding Co.

CET is a Korean company that developed cutting-edge third-generation concentrated photovoltaic cells (CPV) capable of converting more than 44% of solar energy into electricity. It far surpasses silicon and thin-film technologies and has invested billions in global renewable energy R&D.
